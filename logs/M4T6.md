### Backend Changes

**Bug fixes:**
- `passangers` typo → `passengers` (was undeclared variable, causing silent failure)
- Energy consumption formula: was `(energyConsumption / baseRange) * 100` → fixed to `(energyUsed / baseRange) * 100`
- Response keys: `battery_Stress` → `batteryStress`, `energy_Consumption` → `energyConsumption`
- Removed dead `insights` destructuring from `req.body`
- Field names normalized to camelCase: `DrivingCondition` → `drivingCondition`, `battery_size` → `batterySize` etc.

**Smart Insight Engine added (`generateSmartInsights`):**
- Runs after saving each calculation
- Fetches user's last 10 past records from DB
- Requires minimum 3 records before generating trends
- 6 trend checks:
  - Range vs recent average (±15% threshold)
  - Efficiency trend (score diff > 0.5)
  - Battery stress trend (higher than average)
  - Aggressive driving pattern (≥50% of recent trips)
  - Cold weather pattern (≥50% of recent trips below 10°C)
  - Energy consumption trend (>15% above average)
- Merged with Level 1 insights into single array in response
- Frontend `isTrendInsight()` splits them visually into two sections
